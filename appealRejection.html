Appeal Rejection

If your application is rejected, you can appeal the decision. The housing officer will then reconsider your case.

Why do you think that this rejection is not right?

When you appeal, you must explain and prove that this rejection is an incorrect decision.

Additional documents are required for your appeal. Or else, your appeal will more likely be rejected.

Who can appeal?

To appeal, you must be the applicant yourself.


When is the deadline for appealing?

You must appeal within 21 days from the date of being rejected.


How long will it take for your appeal is decided?

The appeal normally will take 2-3 weeks.


How do I appeal?

You can submit additional documents like financial guarantor details, revised income, and other related documents.

You are allowed to appeal once only.

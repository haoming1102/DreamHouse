Application

Single Malaysians with low income can rent micro-homes at as low as RM100 starting August 2019. To rent a room, the eligibility requirements of applicants are as follows:

  • A Malaysian citizen
  • Aged between 18 and 35 years old
  • Earning less than RM2,000 a month
  • Do not own a home
  • Do not own a car
  • Hold a job and work in Kuala Lumpur
